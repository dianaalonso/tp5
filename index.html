The Best Classic Sloppy Joes

Photo by Louis Hansel on Unsplash

What do you need to make the BEST Classic Sloppy Joes?

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup diced yellow onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 lb 90% lean ground beef
  • tomato sauce (8 ounce)
  • 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 6 hamurger buns
  • sliced cheese

Kitchen Equipment

  • Oven
  • Skillet
  • Wooden spoon
  • Spoon to stir
  • Measuring cups
  • Bowl
  • Knife

Directions

  1. Place large skillet over medium-high heat, then add olive oil, bell pepper and cook for 6 min while stirring occationally.
  2. Add the ground beef and break apart with a wooden spoon and cook for 6-8 min, stirring occationally until beef is cooked through.
  3. Add tomatosauce, brown sugar, red wine vinegar, salt, pepper, and stir to combine.
  4. Reduce heat so liquid is smirring (low to medium-low) for 10-12 minutes until sauce thickens.
  5. Remove from heat and let the meat rest for 5 min before serving.
